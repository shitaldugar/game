How to play

players click one of three hand icons by their choice.
.Computer randomly click one of three hand icons.
.Rock which is demonstrated by a fist.
.Scissors with two finger spread out to represent scissors. (This looks like a sideways peace sign.)
.Paper by holding out a palm down, flat hand.
.If players show the same things, they go again.
.If one player picks rock and one scissors, the player who showed rock wins the dispute. To explain this, say rock crushes scissors (no need to actually crush). .If one player picks scissors and the other paper, the player who showed scissors succeeds. Scissors cuts paper. .If a player shows paper while the other shows rock, the player who picked paper succeeds. Paper covers rock.
